Conversational Research Paper Recommender

Discover research papers through a chatbot powered by text classification, clustering, and content-based recommendation.

What this chatbot does

This system helps users explore research papers in areas such as natural language processing, machine learning, recommender systems, databases, information retrieval, and computer vision. It predicts the topic of your query, retrieves relevant papers, and explains why the recommendations were selected.

Topic Prediction

The system classifies your query into a likely research area before retrieving papers.

Paper Recommendation

It finds semantically similar papers and ranks them using a recommendation score.

Explainable Results

You can ask why a paper was recommended, or request more details about a specific result.

Try questions like these

Recommend papers on recommender systems
Find papers about image segmentation and object detection
Show me papers on query optimization
Recommend papers on NLP translation models
Tell me more about paper 1
Why did you recommend paper 2?

Tip: more specific queries usually produce better recommendations than broad queries like “AI”.

Project Context

This chatbot was developed as a final project in Data Science Applications. It combines text preprocessing, TF-IDF feature engineering, classification, clustering, recommendation, and conversational interaction in a single system.
